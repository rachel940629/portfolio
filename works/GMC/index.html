    LG is always looking to improve ThinQ. In the future, we foresee the ability of ThinQ products to adapt to your usage habits

    to work together, delivering a rich, better user experience anywhere, anytime.

  • LG ThinQ plays well with others.

    An Open Platform for Today and Tommorrow

    ing opportunities for the future that include connectivity options, providing easy and convenient coopera-
    tion between a vast array of smart devices. And know that with LG ThinQ, your privacy and security are
    always of the utmost importance. So, while our platform is open, your personal information is not.
